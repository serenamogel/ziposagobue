Who is Jordan Peterson? 

Jordan Bernt Peterson is a Canadian clinical psychologist, professor of psychology at the University of Toronto, author, and cultural critic.

A double B.A. from the University of Alberta, Peterson obtained his Ph.D. in clinical psychology from McGill University.

He attracted international media attention after censoring political correctness and the Canadian government’s Bill C-16 through a series of videos posted on his YouTube channel.

Early Life of Jordan Peterson 

Jordan Peterson on June 12, 1962. He is 60 years of age as of 2022. His zodiac sign is Cancer.

He was born in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada to Walter Peterson and Beverley as the eldest of three children and was raised in Fairview, Alberta. Thus he holds Canadian nationality and belongs to the white ethnicity.

His father was a schoolteacher and his mother was a librarian at the Fairview campus of Grande Prairie Regional College.

The librarian of his school Sandy Notley introduced Peterson to the literary works of Aleksandr Solzhenitsyn, Aldous Huxley, Ayn Rand, and George Orwell.

In 1979 he graduated from Fairview High School and started attending Grande Prairie Regional College to study English literature and political science.

All through his teens, Peterson worked for the New Democratic Party but later got disenchanted and quit the party at age 18.

He left Grande Prairie Regional College in between and transferred to the University of Alberta, from where he obtained his B.A. in Political science in 1982.

He then visited Europe and became interested in the psychological origins of the Cold War, especially in European totalitarianism of the 20th century.

The ability and scope of mankind for doing evil and destruction gradually started bothering him, leading him to go through the works of Fyodor Dostoyevsky, Aleksandr Solzhenitsyn, Friedrich Nietzsche, and Carl Jung.

He went back to the University of Alberta and studied psychology, eventually earning a B.A. degree in the subject in 1984.

He relocated to Montreal in 1985 and furthered his studies at McGill University where he obtained a Ph.D. in clinical psychology in 1991 under the supervision of Robert O. Phil.

His thesis was titled ‘Potential psychological markers for the predisposition to alcoholism’. Thereafter he worked with Phil and Maurice Dongier till June 1993 as a postdoctoral researcher at McGill’s Douglas Hospital.

Jordan Peterson Career

 

Jordan Peterson is a full-time professor at the University of Toronto. However, he has a decent fan following too on social media.

Since September 2016, he posted several videos on his YouTube channel criticizing political correctness and the Canadian government’s Bill C-16.

Such a move of Peterson was censured by many including transgender activists, critics, faculty, and labor unions leading to protests including some violent ones thereby creating controversy and attracting worldwide media attention.

He also received two warning letters from academic administrators at the University of Toronto.

He began his podcast ‘The Jordan B. Peterson Podcast’ in December 2016 and started a series of live theatre lectures ‘The psychological significance of the Biblical stories’ in May 2017.

He has also been featured in several online shows and podcasts like ‘The Rubin Report’, ‘Waking Up’ and ‘The Joe Rogan Experience’.

He has more than two decades of experience in clinical practice attending 20 people weekly. However, he resolved to keep such an endeavor on hold in 2017 to give more time to new projects.

He came up with his second book, ‘12 Rules for Life: An Antidote to Chaos’ published by Penguin Random House in January 2018. The interview went viral on YouTube garnering him considerable attention with over 9 million views.

The book became a best-seller topping bestselling lists in the US, Canada, and the UK including emerging as the #1 best-seller book on Amazon in Canada and the US.

His online presence 

His YouTube channel ‘JordanPetersonVideos’ that he created on March 29, 2013, features his university and public lectures and interviews with people has already amassed over 1 million subscribers and more than 52 million views.

His YouTube channel for shorter videos ‘Jordan B Peterson Clips’ which he created on June 14, 2017, has also accumulated over 66 K subscribers and more than 3.3 million views.
