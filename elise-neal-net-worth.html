Elise Neal Wiki Biography

Elise Neal was born on the 14th March 1966, in Memphis, Tennessee USA. She is known to the world as a television and as well as a film actress. She was also nominated for Best Supporting Actress for the film “Hustle and Low” (2005). Her career as an actress has been active since 1992.

Neal attended Lakeview Elementary, and while still in the elementary school, was taking ballet lessons. She graduated from Overton High School for the Creative and Performing Arts. Elise moved to Philadelphia when she enrolled in the University of the Arts, however, she dropped out after two years and moved to New York to pursue her career in the entertainment industry. The city opened a lot of doors for her, as she acquired roles in musical theatre, and before long, Neal was touring the world. This break allowed her to move to Los Angeles and develop her career further. She earned her first role as Jessica Sinclair in the ABC soap opera “Loving”. In 1993, she earned another role in the NBC`s sci-fi TV series “SeaQuest 2032”.

However, Elise’s most notable role is certainly the one as Yvonne Hughley in the TV series “Hughleys”, which made her famous through the entire acting industry and so earning her auditions for other television and film roles; her career gradually grew and also her net worth. Her film role debut was in the film entitled “Rosewood” (1997), following the success of which Neal was cast in the film “Scream 2”, as Hallie, her most notable film role.

After that film and it`s critical success, the roles just kept coming. Throughout her career, Elise has filmed more than 20 titles, some of which include “Mission to Mars” (2002), “Paid in Full” (2002), “Hustle and Flow” (2005), for which she has been nominated for several awards, including The Best Supporting Actress, “Love Ranch” (2010) and many others, that have had a considerable influence on her net worth.

Elise’s television career is even more successful than her career on the big screen. She has more than 30 titles to her name, the most successful ones are certainly the already mentioned roles in “Hughleys” for which she was nominated several times for the award of “The Best Lead Actress in a comedy series, plus “Loving” and “SeaQuest 2032”, however she has also appeared in episodes of “Law and Order”, “All of Us”, “The Soul Man” and other titles.

Additing to her net worth and career, Neal has also appeared in a number of music videos which include the already mentioned “A Rose is Still a Rose” and “This is it”; she also appeared in “Lisa Baby” performed by Father MC, and “Work to Do” by Vanessa Williams. So far she has a reality show pinned down under her name entitled “Assorted Flavors”. Overall, she has starred in more than 50 titles, rendering her career quite successful.

Regarding her personal life and other interests, she has been dedicated to acting since her first successful audition. Little is known about her private life, just the fact that Neal was engaged to producer F. Gary Gray.
