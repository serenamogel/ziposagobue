Who is Taron Egerton’s ex-girlfriend?

Taron Egerton's ex-girlfriend Emily Thomas is an assistant movie director.

Throughout her career, Emily has worked on the films:

  • Wonder Woman
  • The Batman
  • Justice League
  • No Time to Die
  • Solo: A Star Wars Story

Taron and Emily started dating sometime in 2016.

However, they briefly separated in 2018 and reconciled later in the year.

"Unless you’re really vigilant, you can start to occupy separate worlds," the Rocketman star confessed.
