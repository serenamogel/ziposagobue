Referee: Who Is Harry Milas? His Bio

Late Harry Milas was a renowned football Referee and was a beloved member of our community, known for his compassion, warmth, and infectious personality.

His positive attitude on the pitch, communication style, and dedication led him to be universally respected by coaches, players, and the general public.

Harry started his referee career with Football Victoria in 1998. He held many roles over the 23 years of his service including, Head Instructor, Branch Appointments Officer, and Level 4 Coordinator.

One of the reasons he was loved by many was for his kindness. Even though he was a senior in the field, he would never fail a moment to help the young referees in their growth. He used to even spend his free time and weekends mentoring young officials in assisting them to get through their first game.
