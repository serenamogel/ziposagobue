All the Body Armor Drink Flavors

Publish date: 2024-06-08

If you’re a perpetually dehydrated, very active person, you probably already know this, but there are a lot of Body Armor flavors. When you’re faced with 20-plus bottles of a particular hydration drink, you need to pick a good one and move on with your active-person life. Well, we taste tested every Body Armor flavor we could get our hands on so you can grab the best Body Armor flavor and get back to excessively sweating.

First thing’s first: If you’re not famils, Body Armor is a hydration beverage brand that’s been around since 2011 and is now owned by Coca-Cola. What sets Body Armor apart from, say, Gatorade or Powerade? From what I gather, the answer to that question is that it contains coconut water and antioxidants (in addition to the electrolytes you’ll find in other sports drinks). I’d never had Body Armor prior to this taste test, so I didn’t go into this with any preconceptions or biases. I simply wanted to find out which Body Armor flavors are the tastiest and most gulpable. 

While we set out to taste all Body Armor flavors, there were six we weren’t able to source (you’ll find them listed at the bottom of this post). So we did our best and tasted most Body Armor drink flavors to find the best Body Armor flavor of them all. It’s a weird one! But now I love it!

Body Armor!

Strawberry Lemonade LyteInstacart

When we’re in the office and we taste something really bad, our first impulse tends to be to invite our fellow taste testers to try it, too. I made everyone take a sip of this stuff. As Body Armor flavors go, it’s really bad (most of them are good). Something about it reminded me of the flavor of buttered popcorn, and not in a good way. There are 15 better Body Armor flavors on this list (including lots of Body Armor Lyte flavors) and you should drink those instead.

This Body Armor flavor was another surprise. Frankly, a blue raspberry-flavored hydration drink sounds very bad to me. You imagine it will be like guzzling that candy gel that comes in a tube. Pineapple CoconutInstacart

Body Armor is made with coconut water, so I’m sort of surprised there aren’t more Body Armor flavors that lean into that. Anyway, I’m glad they have this one because it’s really good. It tastes more like Piña Colada mix than coconut water. The pineapple juice flavor is bold, but the coconut is what lingers as you sip. This feels like a treat. If you aren’t all that worried about hydration, hell, go ahead and spike it with some rum.

Orange Mango seems to be a fan favorite Body Armor flavor, and I can see why. It’s not super citrusy in a tart way, it’s citrusy like mandarin oranges and sweet like mango nectar. It’s really interesting and really tasty. A real chugger.

Body Armor!

Strawberry BananaInstacart

Strawberry Banana is the best Body Armor flavor for a couple of reasons. For one thing, I just love the novelty of it. You almost never get to drink strawberry banana-flavored drinks that aren’t thick like juice or a smoothie. This is thin like water but tastes like a smoothie—it’s kind of a mind blower. Plus, the blend of flavors is spot on. The strawberry flavor may have come off as a little artificial, but the banana mellows it out so well. Of all the Body Armor flavors, this is the one I will for sure buy again.
