    Morgan York Facts

  • York was brought up in Los Angeles, California alongside her younger siblings Wendy and Thomas York by her mother Trina.
  • She got her breakthrough as an actress with her 2nd film Cheaper by the Dozen in 2003. Morgan was only 10 years old at the time and she had been cast alongside the likes of various well-known stars including Steve Martin, Bonnie Hunt, Hilary Duff, Tom Welling, and Piper Perabo. Later, in 2005, she appeared alongside actor Vin Diesel in The Pacifier.
  • She had started acting when she was 9 years old.
  • When she started college, she realized that she didn’t want to resume her acting career.
  • In 2010, Morgan announced her retirement from acting after the completion of the 4th season of Hannah Montana.
  • Morgan once stated that the reason behind her departure from the entertainment industry was because she wanted to write adult novels.
  • Morgan is an avid gamer and streams her gaming videos via her Twitch account titled tolstoyce.
  • On October 2, 2015, Morgan began a self-titled YouTube channel. However, it was not until early 2020, that she began uploading videos to it.
  • In her free time, she loves to read books as well as spend time with her beau and family. Also, she enjoys posting content relating to her writing, books, feminism, gaming, LGBTQ issues, diverse fiction, and her acting years on her blog Morgan York Writes.
