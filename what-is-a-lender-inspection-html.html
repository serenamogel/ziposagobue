What is the purpose of a mortgage company doing a home inspection?

Home inspections are performed during the acquisition of a home; property inspections are performed when a homeowner fails on his or her mortgage or is in the process of foreclosure. Your mortgage servicer may conduct monthly property inspections if you fail to make your mortgage payments, in order to guarantee that its interest in the property is preserved.

Is it possible to get an FHA loan without having your home inspected?
