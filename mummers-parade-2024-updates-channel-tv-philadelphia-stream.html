Parade has only been canceled three times

The Mummers Parade has only been canceled three times in its 124-year history.

The parade was called off in 1919 due to the Spanish Flu.

It was canceled in 1934 due to the Great Depression.

Most recently, the parade was not held in 2021 because of the COVID-19 pandemic.
