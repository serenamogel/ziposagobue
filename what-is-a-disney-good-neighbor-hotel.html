What is a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel?

Publish date: 2024-03-07

Learn everything you need to know about Disney Good Neighbor Hotels

If you’re short on time, here’s a quick answer to your question: Disney Good Neighbor Hotels are off-site hotels that meet Disney’s high standards for quality and service.

In this article, we’ll explore what makes a hotel a Disney Good Neighbor, the benefits of staying at one, and how to choose the best one for your next Disney vacation.

What is a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel?

When it comes to finding a place to stay during a Disney vacation, many families turn to Disney Good Neighbor Hotels. But what exactly is a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel? Simply put, it is a hotel that meets Disney’s standards for quality and service, but is not owned or operated by Disney.

These hotels are typically located just off-site from the Disney theme parks, offering a convenient and often more affordable option for families who want to be close to the magic without breaking the bank. Despite not being owned by Disney, these hotels often offer many of the same amenities and perks as on-site Disney resorts.

So, what are the benefits of staying at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel? For starters, many of these hotels offer complimentary shuttle service to the Disney theme parks, making it easy and stress-free to get around. Additionally, some hotels offer special Disney-themed rooms or suites, as well as character breakfasts or other Disney-themed experiences.

Another major benefit of staying at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel is the potential for cost savings. While on-site Disney resorts can be quite expensive, Good Neighbor Hotels often offer more affordable room rates, allowing families to save money without sacrificing quality or convenience.

Keep in mind that while staying at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel can be a great way to save money and enjoy a convenient location, there are some downsides to consider as well. For example, you may not have access to certain Disney perks, such as early park admission or Disney’s Magical Express transportation from the airport.

Ultimately, the decision to stay at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel or an on-site Disney resort comes down to personal preference and budget. Fortunately, there are plenty of options available to suit every family’s needs and preferences.

Disney’s Standards for Quality and Service

Disney’s Good Neighbor Hotels are third-party hotels that meet Disney’s high standards for quality and service. These standards cover several different areas, including:

  • Cleanliness and maintenance: Good Neighbor Hotels must maintain a high level of cleanliness and upkeep to ensure guests have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.
  • Guest services and amenities: These hotels must offer a range of guest services and amenities, such as 24-hour front desk service, complimentary Wi-Fi, and on-site restaurants.
  • Transportation to and from Disney parks: Good Neighbor Hotels must provide transportation options, such as shuttles or buses, to and from the Disney parks to make it easy for guests to get around.
  • Discounted park tickets and packages: These hotels may offer discounted park tickets and vacation packages that include park admission and other amenities.
  • Disney-trained staff: Good Neighbor Hotels are staffed with employees who have been trained by Disney to provide exceptional guest service and create a magical experience for guests.

Off-Site Location

One of the defining features of a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel is its off-site location. These hotels are not located within the Disney parks themselves but are nearby. This location has several benefits for guests, including:

  • Proximity to Disney Parks: Disney Good Neighbor Hotels are located just a few miles away from the Disney parks, making it easy for guests to visit the parks frequently.
  • Ease of Transportation: Many Disney Good Neighbor Hotels offer free shuttle services to the parks, making transportation to and from the parks easier and more convenient for guests.
  • Cost Savings: Staying off-site at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel can be more cost-effective than staying on-site at a Disney resort. Guests can often find better rates and deals at these hotels, allowing them to save money on their trip.

While staying on-site at a Disney resort has its own benefits, staying at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel can be a great option for guests who want to be close to the parks while also saving money on their trip.

Benefits of Staying at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel

Staying at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel can be a great option for those looking to experience the magic of Disney without breaking the bank. Here are some benefits to keep in mind:

  • Lower cost than on-site Disney hotels: While staying on-site at a Disney hotel can be tempting, it can also be quite expensive. Good Neighbor Hotels offer a more affordable option without compromising on the quality of your stay. On average, staying at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel can save you up to 30% compared to on-site Disney hotels.
  • Access to Disney perks and services: Despite not being located on Disney property, Good Neighbor Hotels still offer many of the same perks and services as on-site Disney hotels. Guests can take advantage of complimentary shuttle service to the parks, extended theme park hours, and even character dining experiences.
  • Variety of hotel options: Good Neighbor Hotels come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and price points. From budget-friendly options like the Best Western and Comfort Inn, to more luxurious options like the Waldorf Astoria, there’s a Good Neighbor Hotel for every budget and preference.
  • Flexible booking options: Good Neighbor Hotels offer more flexibility when it comes to booking. Unlike on-site Disney hotels, guests can often book with no deposit and have the option to cancel or modify their reservation without penalty up to 24 hours before their stay.

How to Choose the Best Disney Good Neighbor Hotel

Choosing the best Disney Good Neighbor Hotel can be a daunting task, but it is worth mentioning that there are a few factors to keep in mind to make the process easier.

  • Budget: One of the main considerations when choosing a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel is budget. Fortunately, there are a range of options available to suit different budgets. It is important to take into account the total cost of the stay, including any additional fees and charges.
  • Location: The location of the hotel is also an important factor to consider. Guests may prefer to stay within walking distance of the Disney parks or opt for a hotel that offers a complimentary shuttle service. The proximity of the hotel to other attractions and amenities, such as restaurants and shopping centers, may also be a deciding factor.
  • Amenities: Another important factor to consider when choosing a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel is the amenities that are available. Some hotels offer a range of facilities, such as swimming pools, fitness centers, and on-site restaurants, while others may have more limited offerings. Additionally, families with children may want to look for hotels that offer activities and entertainment for kids.
  • Reviews and ratings: Reviews and ratings from previous guests can provide valuable insight into the quality of a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel. It is important to read a range of reviews to get a comprehensive understanding of the hotel. Look for comments on the cleanliness of the rooms, the quality of the service, and the overall experience of staying at the hotel.

Comparing different Disney Good Neighbor Hotels based on these factors can help guests make an informed decision about which hotel is the best fit for their needs.

Remember, the best Disney Good Neighbor Hotel for one guest may not be the best fit for another. By considering budget, location, amenities, and reviews, guests can find a hotel that meets their individual needs and preferences.

Conclusion

Staying at a Disney Good Neighbor Hotel is a great way to experience the magic of Disney while also enjoying the benefits of an off-site location. By following our guide, you can choose the best hotel for your needs and budget and make the most of your Disney vacation.
