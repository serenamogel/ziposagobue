English professional rugby union player Sam Underhill plays as a flanker for Bath and England National teams. His association with the British professional rugby union club spans for more than 5 years. 

Is Sam Underhill Married?

For those thinking of Sam’s actual relationship status, he is already off the market. He is married to a woman named Josie Underhill with whom he was dating for quite a few years. The grand wedding took place on 17th December 2022 in presence of close friends and family members in Bath, Somerset, England per wiki.


Bath, Somerset has always been a favorite travel destination for the two. Hence, they decided on their favorite destination to exchange their wedding vows. Just four months prior to their grand wedding, the adorable two went on a vacation to Bali, Indonesia. 

His Professional Career: Salary & Injury

The professional athlete earns a lucrative salary of 300,000 Euros per season. His current association has been with Bath since 2017 and prior to that he got the fortune to play for different clubs like Ospreys (2015-2017) and Gloucester (2014-2015) per wiki.


Talking about the National team, he represented England U18 for four 3 years until 2017, then to the senior team. Besides fantastic playing, injury has never been new to him. As per Sky Sports, The Bath forward had to be sidelined for 12 weeks when he suffered a concussion during England’s second Test victory over Australia in Brisbane in July 2022. 

The shoulder surgery took place in late September 2022. 

Sam Underhill Bio & Family: Wiki, Height, University

The player, age 26 was born on 22 July 1996 in Dayton, Ohio per wiki. His family comprises parents Greg Underhill and Olivia Underhill. Besides them, his other family comprises a sister named Rachel Underhill. Both his sister and her boyfriend Mike Connelly are currently working at Gloucester Royal Hospital.
