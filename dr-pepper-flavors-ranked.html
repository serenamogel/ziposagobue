Dr Pepper Flavors, Ranked [Taste Test]

Publish date: 2024-06-07

The best Dr Pepper flavor tastes like Dr Pepper—duh!

Famously, Dr Pepper managed to combine 23 flavors to create the perfect beverage. I am a Dr Pepper devotee. I would drink it in place of water if I could. To me, it is truly the most delicious beverage on the face of planet earth. While I’m a purist and typically stick to the classic Dr Pepper, there are many other Dr Pepper flavors to wet your whistle, from Strawberries & Cream to Cherry. We tried every Dr Pepper flavor currently on the market and ranked them from worst to best. Dr Pepper drinkers, scroll away!

Dr Pepper!

Dr Pepper Strawberries & CreamWalmart

Strawberries & Cream Dr Pepper is the newest addition to the lineup of Dr Pepper flavors. Unfortunately, it doesn’t taste anything like Dr Pepper at all, so I don’t really see the point. It tastes like a very strong strawberry soda. If you’re a strawberry fan, check it out. If you’re looking for Dr Pepper, leave this one on the shelf.

Diet Dr PepperTarget

Lots of people say Diet Dr Pepper is the best diet soda of all time because it tastes most similar to its non-diet counterpart. I agreed with this until I tried the zero sugar Dr Peppers. This still isn’t bad, but I don’t know why you’d buy it when the zeros are so much better. That being said, I still respect all the Diet Dr Pepper stans out there.

Dr Pepper & Cream Soda Zero SugarWalmart

If you like cream soda, you like Dr Pepper, and you want a diet soda, you’ll like this. The taste is slightly different than the regular Cream Soda Dr Pepper and it does have a little bit of a chemical finish, but it’s certainly not bad. I think people who regularly drink diet soda would love this.

Dr Pepper CherryWalmart

I don’t totally get Cherry Dr Pepper, but I respect it. In my opinion, Dr Pepper already has enough cherry. When you add in more cherry, it offsets the balance a little. It becomes Cherry Dr Pepper when maybe it should be Dr Pepper Cherry. It does taste really good, though! That’s why it’s the fourth best Dr Pepper flavor we tried.

This is one of the Dr Pepper flavors that I would buy with my own money and consume again.

Dr Pepper Zero SugarTarget

Dr Pepper Zero Sugar is the best diet soda because it tastes almost exactly like normal Dr Pepper. I absolutely hate diet soda and was shocked by the similarities between this and Dr Pepper made with sugar. There’s a very minimal diet-beverage sort of taste, and I think you might not even notice it if the soda was served freezing cold over ice. I would drink this, and I can’t really say that about any other diet soda on the market.
